== Description ==

= What is Zoho Forms = 

[Zoho Forms](https://www.zoho.com/forms?src=wordpress) is a powerful no-code form builder that lets you create and embed beautiful, functional forms on your WordPress site in minutes. Whether you’re looking to build contact forms, order forms, subscription forms, or payment forms, Zoho Forms makes it simple. Its intuitive drag-and-drop interface ensures that anyone—from beginners to expert developers—can create professional forms effortlessly, without writing a single line of code.

= Why Choose Zoho Forms for WordPress? =

Zoho Forms integrates directly with [WordPress](https://www.zoho.com/forms/wordpress-forms.html?src=wordpress), making it the perfect tool for building, customizing, and embedding responsive forms that adapt to all devices and browsers. With features like lead tracking, payment processing, and workflow automation, Zoho Forms enhances user engagement, lead generation, and customer interactions—all from your WordPress site.

= Configuring your Wordpress form =

Here's a step-by-step guide that will help you configure Zoho Forms for Wordpress.

1. Sign up or log in to your [Zoho Forms](http://forms.zoho.com) account and create a form that suits your requirements.
2. To add your Zoho Form to a WordPress page or post, click the <strong>+ (Block Inserter)</strong> icon in the top left corner of your screen. In the search bar, type <strong>Zoho Forms</strong> and select the block from the search results.
3. Once the plugin is added, click <strong>Access Zoho Forms</strong> to select an existing form or choose <strong>Embed using permalink</strong> to enter the [form’s permalink](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/sharing-forms/articles/sharing-a-form-publicly#Sharing_a_link).
4. After selecting your form, you'll see two embedding options: <strong>iFrame</strong> or <strong>JavaScript</strong>.
5. If you choose the iFrame option, you can customize the form container's width and height.
6. If you select the JavaScript option, the form container's width can be set manually, while the height will automatically adjust based on the number of form fields.
7. In the end, you can also add <strong>parameters</strong> to your form's URL to track form submissions or prefill form fields. [Click here](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/form-settings/prefill/articles/field-alias#What_is_prefilling_form_fields) to learn more about the URL parameters in Zoho Forms.

= Embedding forms in Wordpress using Shortcode =

Zoho Forms plugins are compatible with both the block editor and the classic editor plugin in WordPress. To embed in third-party editors like Elementor, SeedProd and more, you can make use of <strong>shortcode block/widget</strong>. 

1. To add a shortcode block to your Wordpress page, simply click the <strong>+ (Block Inserter)</strong> icon in the top left corner of your screen. In the search bar ,type <strong>Shortcode</strong> and select the block from the search results.
2. You can then use the following shortcode format to embed your Zoho Form on your website. 

> [zohoForms src=<permalink> width=100% height=600px urlparams=”” type=<js/iframe> autoheight=<true/false>]

[Click here](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/embedding-forms/embedding-forms-on-website-builders/articles/embedding-forms-on-a-wordpress-website#Embed_using_Short_Code) to learn more about appropriate shortcode formats.

Note: You can copy the form permalink from the [Share tab](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/sharing-forms/articles/sharing-a-form-publicly#Sharing_a_link) in your form builder. 

These setup instructions are for WordPress versions 5.0 and above. 
[Click here](https://www.zoho.com/forms/help/wordpress-plugin.html#WordPress-4.9.9) to learn more about Zoho Forms plugin for WordPress 4.9.9 & below.


== Installation ==

You can install the Zoho Forms plugin in two ways:

= Install from the WordPress plugin installer = 

1. In your WordPress site, Navigate to the <strong>Plugins</strong> tab and then click on <strong>Add New Plugin</strong> located just below.
2. Search for <strong>Zoho Forms</strong> in the search bar and then click on <strong>Install Now</strong>.
3. After installing the plugin, click on the <strong>Activate</strong> button. You can then find Zoho Forms under the <strong>Installed Plugins</strong> section.

= Download the plugin = 

1. You can also download <strong>Zoho Forms plugin</strong> from the <strong>Plugin Homepage</strong>.
2. After downloading the plugin into your system, upload it to the folder, "/wp-content/plugins/zohoforms/" and <strong>activate</strong> the plugin. 

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 1. Who should use Zoho Forms? =

Zoho Forms is perfect for businesses of any kind/size, who needs an easy-to-use tool to collect and manage data efficiently.

= 2. What types of forms can I build with Zoho Forms? = 

With Zoho Forms, you can create:
<strong>Standard Forms</strong>: Ideal for registrations, applications, and [lead generation](https://www.zoho.com/forms/lead-generation-forms.html).
<strong>Card Forms</strong>: [Interactive](https://www.zoho.com/forms/card-forms.html), one-question-at-a-time forms for an engaging user experience.
<strong>CRM Forms</strong>: Directly [sync data](https://www.zoho.com/forms/crm-forms.html) with Zoho CRM, making data flow effortless.
You can also choose from a variety of [templates](https://www.zoho.com/forms/templates.html) like contact forms, HR forms, order forms, and more to get started quickly.

= 3. Can I collect payment through Zoho Forms? = 

Yes, Zoho Forms offers over [10 payment gateway](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/payment-options) integrations that allow secure transactions directly through your forms.

= 4. Can I send email notifications with Zoho Forms? = 

Zoho Forms provides an [email notification](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/form-settings/notifications/email-notifications/articles/email-notifications-overview) feature that ensures real-time alerts for each form submission. It also enables you to keep respondents informed with personalized emails, making response management seamless and efficient.


= 5. Is my data secure with Zoho Forms? = 

Yes, your data is secure with Zoho Forms. We prioritize data security, implementing robust measures to safeguard your information. Key security features include compliance with global standards like HIPAA, GDPR, and CCPA, along with Spam control, Encrypted fields, and Detailed record auditing to protect against unauthorized access.

For a comprehensive overview of our data protection measures, visit our [Secure Forms](https://www.zoho.com/forms/secure-forms.html) page.

= 6. How do I embed Zoho Forms while using third party editors like  Elementor, Divi builder, SeedProd , etc..? = 

When embedding your form in a third-party editor, you have the option to utilize a [Shortcode](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/embedding-forms/embedding-forms-on-website-builders/articles/embedding-forms-on-a-wordpress-website#Embed_using_Short_Code) block/widget. Alternatively, you can also embed the form using its [iframe code](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/embedding-forms/embedding-forms-in-webpages/articles/embed-using-iframe) on your page.

= 7. Can I add tracking codes to a Zoho Form in my Wordpress page? =

Yes, you can add tracking codes to a Zoho Form. Copy the [UTM tracking code](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/forms/tracking-in-zoho-forms/overview/articles/choose-the-right-tracking#Tracking_with_UTM_Parameters) from the Share tab in Zoho Forms, then paste the code into the footer section of your webpage to enable tracking.

= 8. How can I remove the scroll from my embedded form? = 

If your embedded form contains a scroll, it might be due to an incorrect height setting in the iframe option. Alternatively, using the JavaScript embed option can automatically adjust the height to fit your form.

= 9. Why is my Zoho Forms plugin not working or unclickable? =

If the Zoho Forms plugin appears unclickable or doesn't work as expected, try disabling the Gutenberg editor and any other  add-on plugins. This can often resolve compatibility issues.

= 10. How do I customize my form's appearance on the Wordpress page? = 

To customize the appearance of your form on a WordPress page, go to the Themes tab in your Zoho Forms builder. You can either select from the available themes or create a personalized form design to match your preferences.
